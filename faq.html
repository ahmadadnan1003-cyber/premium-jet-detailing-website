FAQ

Aircraft interior care questions.

Short answers for private jet detailing, aircraft interior care and business aviation cabin support around Berlin, BER and Germany on request.

Who provides private jet interior cleaning in Berlin?

Premium Jet Detailing provides aircraft interior care and private jet detailing for business aviation in Berlin, BER and surrounding areas.

Does Premium Jet Detailing offer aircraft interior care at BER?

Yes. Requests can be reviewed once aircraft location, access requirements, service scope and timing are confirmed.

How is access at BER coordinated?

For BER, access must be coordinated through the aircraft's handling agent, FBO or an authorized airport-based company.

Are services available outside Berlin?

Requests across Germany can be reviewed individually depending on aircraft location, access requirements, service scope and timing.

What is the difference between aircraft cleaning and aircraft detailing?

Cleaning focuses on general cabin cleanliness. Detailing is more precise and includes material care, touchpoints and refined presentation.

Do you clean leather seats in private jets?

Yes. Private jet leather care can be requested for seats, armrests and passenger contact areas depending on material condition.

Do you clean aircraft carpets and upholstery?

Yes. Aircraft carpet cleaning and upholstery care can be included depending on material type, condition and agreed scope.

Is cockpit or avionics cleaning included?

Cockpit or avionics cleaning is not automatically included and must be discussed separately with clear authorization and scope.

How are prices calculated?

Prices depend on aircraft size, cabin condition, location, access window, materials and required service level.
