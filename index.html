Why Premium Jet Detailing

Aircraft interior care, clearly focused.

Premium Jet Detailing is focused on aircraft interior care for private jets, business jets and executive aircraft in Berlin / BER. The service approach is material-safe, discreet and aviation-focused, covering cabin refresh, leather care, carpet and textile care, galley care, lavatory care, display care and high-touch surface care. Service requests at BER are coordinated through the aircraft's handling partner, FBO or another authorized airport-based party. Requests across Germany can be reviewed individually depending on aircraft location, access requirements, timing and scope of work.
