Safety & Compliance for Aircraft Interior Cleaning

Clear service boundaries for aircraft interior cleaning, private jet detailing and business aviation cabin care.

Safety and compliance are central to the way Premium Jet Detailing UG positions its aircraft interior cleaning and detailing services. The company provides cleaning and detailing services only. It does not perform aircraft maintenance, technical work, repairs, disassembly, modification or work on systems that belong to aircraft operation, safety or certification.

The service scope is limited to approved cabin cleaning and detailing activities. That may include passenger-facing areas such as leather seats, carpets, upholstery, tables, side panels, lavatory surfaces, galley contact areas, glass, mirrors and high-touch surfaces. Work on avionics, flight controls, sensors, engines, cockpit controls, seat mechanisms, safety belts or other safety-critical components is not included unless explicit operator instruction defines a safe cleaning boundary, and technical responsibility remains with the operator or authorized personnel.

All work is carried out subject to operator, handler, owner and airport requirements. At BER or other airport locations, access may require an official request or assignment from the aircraft owner, operator, charter company, handler, FBO or another authorized airport-based party. Premium Jet Detailing UG does not claim to be an official airport partner or maintenance provider.

Before and after documentation can be used where appropriate to support transparency around visible cleaning and detailing work. Products and methods are selected carefully for aircraft interiors and should match the approved scope, cabin condition and material suitability. The objective is premium presentation and material-conscious care, not technical intervention.

Exterior cleaning and dry wash may be offered in the future once all required approvals, operational requirements and insurance conditions are fulfilled. Until then, the current focus remains aircraft interior cleaning, aircraft cabin cleaning, private jet detailing, leather care, carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, lavatory detailing and high-touch surface care.
