Aircraft carpet cleaning and upholstery cleaning require controlled methods, careful material handling and a realistic understanding of cabin use. Premium Jet Detailing UG supports private jets, business aircraft and executive aviation cabins with carpet spot cleaning, textile care, selected upholstery cleaning and cabin presentation in Berlin, BER, EDAV Finow, Brandenburg and Germany on request.

Carpets and textiles often show the practical side of aircraft operations. Entry areas, seating zones and walkways can collect marks, particles and localized staining. Upholstery and soft trim may need careful refreshing without over-wetting, harsh chemicals or rushed methods. The right approach depends on the material condition, the aircraft schedule and the agreed level of cleaning.

A carpet and upholstery scope may include vacuuming, selected spot treatment, careful soft-material refresh and attention to passenger-facing textiles. It can be paired with aircraft interior cleaning, aircraft leather cleaning, lavatory detailing, galley surface cleaning and high-touch surface cleaning. The aim is not to overpromise impossible restoration, but to improve presentation and support a premium cabin feel.

For operators, aircraft management companies and charter teams, soft-material care can support consistent cabin standards. For owners and brokers, it can help the aircraft feel better prepared for passengers, viewings or handover activity. Around BER and other airport locations, the service is reviewed around access requirements and the available ground window.

Premium Jet Detailing UG provides cleaning and detailing services only. We do not perform technical work, maintenance, repairs or disassembly. All work is carried out subject to operator, handler, owner and airport requirements.

For a realistic quote, share the aircraft type, carpet or upholstery concern, location and preferred time window through the request quote page. Related services include aircraft leather cleaning and aircraft interior cleaning Berlin.
