FAQ

Aircraft interior care questions.

Short answers for private jet detailing, aircraft cabin cleaning and business aviation support around Berlin / BER.

Do you clean private jets at BER?

Yes, we provide aircraft interior care support in the Berlin/BER area. For airside work at BER, access usually needs to be coordinated through an official request or assignment from the aircraft owner, operator, charter company or handling partner.

How far in advance should a BER request be made?

Ideally 24-48 hours in advance, depending on aircraft location, handling coordination, airport access requirements and requested service scope. Short-notice requests can be reviewed individually.

Do you work with aircraft owners, operators and handling partners?

Yes. We support private aircraft owners, business jet operators, charter companies, aircraft management companies and handling partners.

What services do you provide?

Services include cabin cleaning, leather care, carpet and upholstery cleaning, galley and lavatory cleaning, glass and mirror cleaning, surface care and on-demand turnaround interior support.

Do you provide fixed prices?

We provide tailored quotations. Pricing depends on aircraft type, cabin size, condition level, access requirements, time window and requested scope of work.

Do you clean cockpit or avionics areas?

Cockpit, avionics, seat mechanics, belts, switches and other safety-sensitive areas are only handled with explicit approval and according to operator requirements.

Are you available outside BER?

Premium Jet Detailing is based in Berlin. Requests in the Berlin-Brandenburg area and other German locations can be reviewed on request.

Can you support short-notice turnaround cleaning?

Yes, short-notice support may be possible depending on location, access requirements, aircraft schedule and available working time.
